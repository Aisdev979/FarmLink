How FarmLink Works

Simple, secure, and straightforward process to connect farmers and
buyers across Nigeria.

For Farmers

Register & Verify

Create your account and submit your NIN for admin
verification. Upload your profile photo and farm details.

List Your Products

Add your farm produce with detailed descriptions, prices,
quantities, and high-quality photos.

Connect with Buyers

Receive direct calls and WhatsApp messages from verified
buyers interested in your products.

Track Performance

Monitor your listings' performance, view insights, and
optimize your offerings.

For Buyers

Register & Verify

Sign up with your details and NIN for verification.
Get approved to access our marketplace.

Browse Products

Search and filter through thousands of products from
verified farmers across Nigeria.

Contact Farmers

Call or WhatsApp farmers directly to discuss prices,
quantities, and delivery arrangements.

Rate & Review

Leave ratings and reviews to help other buyers and
support quality farmers.
